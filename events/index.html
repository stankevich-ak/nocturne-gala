FAQ

Before you join the ball

Common questions about rehearsals, tickets, dress code, and event format.

Do I need dance experience?

No. Our events are designed to be beginner-friendly, and the dances are taught before the ball.

How do I learn the dances?

We provide classes, rehearsals, or workshops before the ball so guests can learn the dances, meet each other, and feel comfortable before the evening begins.

Can I come alone?

Yes. You can come alone with the right ticket type. Ticket availability may depend on dance role balance for that event.

Leader TicketFor one guest dancing the leading role.
Follower TicketFor one guest dancing the following role.
Couples TicketFor two guests registering together.

Is this historically accurate?

We use beginner-friendly adaptations of actual historic dances. The goal is to make the dances feel elegant, social, and accessible rather than strict reenactment.

Are rehearsals included in the ticket?

Yes. The rehearsals, classes, or workshops listed for the event are included in the ticket.

What should I wear?

It depends on the theme of the ball. We provide costume inspiration for each event so guests can plan formalwear or theme-appropriate looks.

Is there an age restriction?

Yes. Events are 14 plus. Guests under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.

What is the refund policy?

Ticket sales are final, but ticket transfers are allowed before the start of rehearsals.
