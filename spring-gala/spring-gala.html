About the Evening

An elegant night of dance, connection, and romance.

The Spring Ball was Nocturne Gala's first event — a full historic dance evening for beginners and curious newcomers, built around four Tuesday-evening rehearsals and a final evening of dancing at 4585 Albert St in Burnaby.

Guests arrived recognizing faces. The music started and the steps came naturally. Live violin wove through every dance of the evening.

The only thing between you and this night was one decision.
