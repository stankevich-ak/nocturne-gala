Rehearsals

Learn Dances Before the Ball

Before the Spring Gala, we met for five guided rehearsals and built the evening step by step. The goal was simple: make the dances feel familiar, social, and beginner-friendly before the final night.

We focused on historic formations, partner changes, timing, and enough repetition for people to start recognizing the room around them.

Here are the dances we’ve learned:

  • Polka for Three
  • Spanish Waltz
  • Waltz
  • Cotillon
  • Minuet
  • Polonaise
The Ball

Dancing, live music, games, and spring memories.

We met lots of people, danced, ate, played, and made the room feel alive.

The evening had various dances, live music, historic games, a King and Queen contest, sword combat and sparring entertainment, lots of dancing, and a little bit of playful nonsense in the best way.
