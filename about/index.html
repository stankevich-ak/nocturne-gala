About Nocturne Gala

Not just a party.
An initiation.

Nocturne Gala creates structured, immersive historic dance balls across British Columbia. We don't just throw elegant evenings — we build the experience from the ground up, starting with you.

Every event includes guided workshops or rehearsals so guests learn the dances, meet other people, and arrive at the ball already feeling at home. By the time the music starts, the room is no longer anonymous.

You've been watching scenes like this your whole life. This is your chance to step out of it.
  • Beginner-friendly — no dance experience needed
  • Structured program with guided instruction
  • Live music, photography, and beautiful venues
  • Social from the very beginning
